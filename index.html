{Something} Labs

A place for my side projects.

Drawstring

Surrealist communal comic strips.

Inspired by the 100 year old surrealist game, exquisite corpse, Drawstring lets you create comic strips with your friends. Each person draws one of the four panels, but can only see the panel that comes right before them. Nobody sees the full picture until it's done.

Superscript

Distraction-free comic script editor.

Don't worry about numbering pages and panels. Forget about switching between headings, action, characters and dialog. Just write and let Superscript handle the rest.

Summer

Simple scorekeeper app.

Forget pen and paper? Too drunk to add numbers? Open up Summer to keep score of your card and board games.

Seconds

Multiple cooking timers on your phone.

Dinner on time. Easily create multiple timers to make every piece of your meal ready right on time.

Genome

Yet another node builder.

Simple library for compiling your front-end files. No propriety syntax to learn. Just use plain Javascript.
