Drawstring

Create surrealist comic strips with your friends and strangers.

Inspired by the 100 year old surrealist game, exquisite corpse, Drawstring lets you create comic strips with your friends. Each person draws one of the four panels, but can only see the panel that comes right before them. Nobody sees the full picture until it's done.

Superscript

Distraction-free comic script editor.

Don't worry about numbering pages and panels. Forget about switching between headings, action, characters and dialog. Just write and let Superscript handle the rest.

The syntax is inspired by markdown. One hastag creates a page heading. Two hashtags create a panel heading. Superscript inserts the page and panel numbers automatically, as you type.
